About the Authors

Shreya R. G. Kanade

Born in Maharashtra, India (2008) — Shreya is the author of MayFalls (2023) and creator of SuperFriendly10. A poet, lyricist, and storyteller of magical realism, she studies Management & Digital Marketing at Seth Hirachand Mutha College, with dreams of pursuing Law. Her words drift between reality and reverie — where emotion becomes eternity.

Shruti G. Kanade

Born in Maharashtra, India (2010) — Shruti is a poetess and co-author of the SuperFriendly10 saga. With a heart tuned to romance and emotion, she turns feelings into verses that linger like echoes. Together with her sister, she builds worlds spun from heartbeats and hope.

Author’s Note

Two hearts, one dream — to breathe life into ink. From WordsCraft.Mirage flows a realm where stories shimmer, and friendship, love, and fate entwine like constellations. Every word you read — a whisper of something eternal.
