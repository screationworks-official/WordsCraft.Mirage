Superfriendly 10: Five Friends Adventure

Season 1 — Where It All Began

A friendship forged in the laughter of childhood. A promise made under an ordinary sun. James, Shally, Jenny, George, and Chase — five souls intertwined by fate, unaware that the world they knew was but a whisper of something far greater.

Through laughter, quarrels, and the trials of growing up, their bond is tested — until one day, reality cracks open and reveals a spark of the extraordinary. Season 1 sets the stage for the legend of *Superfriendly 10* — the home where hearts learn to heal and powers begin to awaken.
